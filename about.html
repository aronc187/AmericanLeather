About Our Company

We are the leaders of corporate entertainment shoe-service in America. We will always endeavor to produce the functional expertise, development, production capabilities, and customer service skills beyond anyone in our industry.

We are craftsman... We are fixers... We are artisans...and we enjoy this type of work! While other shoe companies mass-produce; We mass-repair! We stand behind our products & services, and will do everything possible to please our clients.

Essentially, if it can be done to a shoe, costume, handbag, or garment, we will perform it! The USA Shoe Company is the American made, get it rebuilt society. We offer premier Shoe Service Solutions to everyone, no matter how big or small the job may be. Beyond that, we've developed a Philanthropic Division to donate shoes to the less fortunate members of our community. We are the home town company that supports non-profit.
